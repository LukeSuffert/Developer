Just Breathe

pause and find your peace. This page offers simple, guided steps to help you reconnect with yourself. Whether you're outside, in a quiet room, or navigating a busy space, these short meditations are designed to meet you where you are, gently grounding you in the present moment.

Open Environments

  1. Pause & Relax (where are you? List in your mind everything you see)
  2. Ground Yourself (Feel your feet on the ground, connect to the earth)
  3. Inhale Slowly (4 seconds)
  4. Exhale Gently (6 seconds)
  5. Repeat 3–5 times

Public Closed Environments

  1. Find a comfort spot (adjust your posture and find a nice position)
  2. Notice your breathing
  3. Silent count (inhale for 4s, hold for 2s, exhale for 6s)
  4. Use the moment (let sounds around you guide your breath like waves)
  5. Don’t be afraid to practice anytime, anywhere

Private Closed Environments

  1. Set the mood (dim lights, light a candle, or open a window)
  2. Choose a posture (sit or lie comfortably and feel supported)
  3. Box Breathing 4–4–4–4 (inhale, hold, exhale, hold for 4s each)
  4. End gently (return to the room, stretch, and thank yourself)
