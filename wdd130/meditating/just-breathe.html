Just Breathe

pause and find your peace. This page offers simple, guided steps to help you reconnect with yourself. Whether you're outside, in a quiet room, or navigating a busy space, these short meditations are designed to meet you where you are, gently grounding you in the present moment.

Open environments

1 - Pause & Relax (where are you? List in your mind everything you see); 2 - Ground Yourself (Feel your feet on the ground, connect to the earth); 3 - Inhale Slowly (4 seconds); 4 - Exhale Gently (6 seconds); 5 - Repeat 3-5 times.

Public Closed Environments

1 - Find a comfort spot (adjust your posture and find a nice position); 2 - Notice your breathing; 3 - Silent count (count 4 seconds on inhale, hold for 2 seconds, and exhale for 6 seconds); 4 - Use the Moment (use the sounds around you as pacing, let them go like waves as you breathe); 5 - Don't be afraid to practice anytime, anywhere.

Private Closed Environments

1 - Set the mood (dim the lights or add a calming element — a candle, soft music, or an open window); 2 - Choose a posture (sit or lie down comfortably, ensuring your body feels supported); 3 - Box Breathing 4–4–4–4 (Inhale, Hold your breath, Exhale and Hold for 4 seconds each. Repeat for several cycles); 4 - End Gently (When you're ready, return your attention to the room. Stretch lightly, and thank yourself for your effort).
